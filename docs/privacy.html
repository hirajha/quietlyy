Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026  |  Last updated: April 6, 2026

Quietlyy ("we", "our", or "us") operates an automated social media content pipeline that generates and publishes short-form video content to Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube on behalf of the Quietlyy brand. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in connection with our Meta app integration.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from end users. Our application only accesses the Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account that it is authorised to manage, using tokens granted by the page administrator.

2. How We Use Information

  • To publish video content to the Quietlyy Facebook Page and Instagram account.
  • To read engagement metrics (likes, views) for the content we post.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not store user data. Access tokens are stored as encrypted GitHub Actions secrets and are never logged or transmitted to any third-party service.

4. Third-Party Services

Our pipeline uses the following third-party APIs: Meta Graph API, ElevenLabs (text-to-speech), OpenAI / Google Gemini (script generation), and Freesound (background music). Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

This application does not collect or store personal data from any user other than the authorised page administrator. To request removal of any data or to revoke app access, contact us at hira.d.jha@gmail.com or remove the app from your Meta Business settings.

6. Contact

For privacy questions: hira.d.jha@gmail.com
